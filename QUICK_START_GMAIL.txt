STEPS:

2. Find transaction email
3. Copy entire email (Ctrl+A, Ctrl+C)
4. Click "📥 Import Transactions" in app
5. Go to "📧 Email Import" tab
6. Paste email content
7. Click "🔍 Parse Email"
8. Done!
